Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. Always.

Last updated: January 2026

Email Converter ("the App", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application, available at mbox2eml.com.

TL;DR: We collect zero personal data. All email processing happens locally on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics.

Information We Collect

None. Email Converter does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Email content, attachments, or metadata
  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Usage statistics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • IP addresses

How the App Works

When you use Email Converter:

  • Local Processing Only: All file conversion happens entirely on your Mac
  • No Internet Required: The app works completely offline
  • No Data Transmission: Your emails never leave your computer
  • No Cloud Storage: We have no servers that receive your data

Local Data Storage

The App stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Window preferences: Position and size (standard macOS user defaults)
  • Usage counter: A simple number to determine when to show the "rate this app" prompt

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by removing the app.

Third-Party Services

Email Converter does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media SDKs

External links on this website (App Store, feedback form, support page) open in your browser and are subject to those services' privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Email Converter does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). Since no data is collected, no age verification is required.

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have rights regarding your personal data. Since Email Converter collects no personal data:

  • Right to Access: There is no data to access
  • Right to Rectification: There is no data to correct
  • Right to Erasure: There is no data to delete
  • Right to Data Portability: There is no data to export

Your email files remain entirely under your control on your own device.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • We do not sell personal information
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not share personal information with third parties

Data Security

Because all processing occurs locally on your device with no data transmission, your information is protected by your own device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using FileVault disk encryption on your Mac
  • Keeping your macOS updated
  • Securing your converted EML files appropriately

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Material changes will be noted on our website.
